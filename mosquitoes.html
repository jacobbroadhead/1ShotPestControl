    Did you know...?

    Disease Transmission

    Mosquitoes are vectors for serious diseases such as West Nile virus, Zika virus, dengue fever, and malaria. These diseases can have severe health impacts on humans and pets, making mosquito control crucial for safety.

     

    Biting & Irritation

    Mosquito bites can cause itchy, red welts that can be very uncomfortable. For individuals with allergies to mosquito saliva, bites can lead to more severe skin reactions and infections from scratching.

     

    Pet Health

    Mosquitoes can transmit heartworm to dogs and cats, which is a potentially fatal disease if not treated. Protecting pets from mosquitoes is an important aspect of responsible pet ownership.
