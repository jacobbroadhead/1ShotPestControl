    Did you know...?

    Landscaping Descrution

    Gophers feed on a variety of plants, including grasses, flowers, shrubs, and vegetables. Their feeding habits can decimate gardens and landscaping, causing frustration for homeowners trying to maintain a beautiful yard.

     

    Tunnel Networks

    Gophers create complex tunnel systems that can stretch hundreds of feet in length. These tunnels can disrupt irrigation systems, making it difficult to maintain a healthy lawn or garden.

     

    Erosion Problems

    The extensive burrowing activity of gophers can lead to soil erosion, particularly on slopes and hillsides. This erosion can result in unsightly landscapes and increased maintenance costs.
