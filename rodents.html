    Did you know...?

    Disease Transmission

    Mosquitoes can transmit heartworm to dogs and cats, which is a potentially fatal disease if not treated. Protecting pets from mosquitoes is an important aspect of responsible pet ownership.

     

    Nesting Habbits

    Rodents build nests in hidden areas such as wall cavities, attics, and basements using shredded materials like paper, fabric, and insulation. These nests can be difficult to locate and remove.

     

    Rapid Reproduction

    Rodents breed quickly, with some species like mice capable of producing a new litter every three weeks. A small infestation can rapidly grow into a major problem if not addressed promptly.
