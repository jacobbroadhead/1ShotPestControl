    Defending your home and property against unwanted birds.

    Did you know...?

    Fire Hazards

    Birds often build their nests in and around electrical equipment and machinery, creating fire hazards. Nests made of dry twigs and grass can easily catch fire, leading to potential property damage.

     

    Health Concerns

    Bird droppings and nesting materials can harbor mites, ticks, and fleas, which can infest homes and businesses, posing health risks to humans and pets.

     

    Property Damage

    Birds such as pigeons, starlings, and seagulls can cause significant damage to buildings and infrastructure. Their droppings are acidic and can corrode building materials, leading to costly repairs.
