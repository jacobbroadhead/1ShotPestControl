    Did you know...?

    Stinging Insects

    Wasps and hornets are flying insects known for their painful stings, which can cause allergic reactions and severe discomfort in humans. Unlike bees, which can only sting once before dying, wasps and hornets can sting multiple times.

     

    Aggressive Defense

    Wasps and hornets are territorial and will aggressively defend their nests if they perceive a threat. Disturbing or approaching a nest can trigger an attack, putting individuals at risk of multiple stings.

     

    Allergic Reactions

    For individuals allergic to insect stings, encounters with wasps and hornets can be life-threatening. Anaphylaxis, a severe allergic reaction, can occur rapidly and requires immediate medical attention.
